## Task 1 — Data Visualization 

## Objective
Create a bar chart or histogram to visualize the distribution of a categorical or continuous variable, such as the distribution of ages or genders in a population.

## Dataset
World Bank Population Dataset (`API_SP.POP.TOTL`) — country-wise total population data.

## Approach
- Loaded and cleaned the dataset (handled missing values, removed aggregate region rows)
- Built a histogram to show the distribution of population sizes across countries
- Built a bar chart to show the top 10 most populous countries

## Tools Used
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib
